Overview

During the intense period of COVID19 pandemic, multiple companies and individuals banded together to produce a large quantity of face shields to help protect the front line workers. The capabilities at CSIRO, allowed for a mass production of face masks to be fabricated quickly, with the final production around 3000 face masks in less than a week. The work perfomed here by the team was noticed by CSIRO and publicly announced in this post and article.
