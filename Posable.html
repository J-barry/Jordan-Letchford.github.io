Overview

This project was based on the work of Tim Hojnik, his work can be seen here. To help demonstrate his findings, I was tasked to build a smaller replica of his wheel design which would allow him to showcase the working functionality of the project and highlight the capabilities of "one-shot" 3D printing on the Stratasys Connex3.
