Overview

During University, we partake in various projects to help solidify our knowledge and skills. My most memorable projects were in my Mechatronics units as I was able to physically build robots. These projects required 3D design, PCB design, 3D printing and laser cutting, programming of microcontrollers, vision, report writing, problem solving and collaborative team work to complete. Additionally, I covered SLAM, autonomous systems, modern control and embedded systems, all of which are relative and useful in today's robotic world.

Besides University, I participated in numerous societies and clubs where countless projects were built, too many to cover.
